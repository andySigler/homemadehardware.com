Overview

Hardware is not hard, and rapidly prototyping circuit boards is easier than ever with new tools at ITP. This class is about artists and designers taking control of their hardware, and exploring the potential of embedding their projects into the world around them.

There will be roughly three projects made in this course, tentatively due weeks 5, 9, and 14. It is production heavy, with the first half of the semester dedicated to learning new processes on concepts towards making PCBs on the floor. The second half will be focus on individual projects and how to structure their design and development.

This is a 14-week course, meeting every Thursday from 6:30-9:00pm, with no class on November 26th during Thanksgiving vacation.
